## Overview

Regional Corporate Technologies (RCT) is a leading software and technology solutions company based in South Sudan, with a presence across the African continent. The company is dedicated to providing innovative and cutting-edge solutions to meet the diverse needs of businesses in Africa. The design of RCT's logo reflects this commitment to simplicity and creativity, reflecting the company's philosophy of delivering practical, efficient, and effective technology solutions.

The abstract and clean design of the logo is inspired by traditional African patterns and incorporates the company's initials r, c, and t. Additionally, the logo features an icon of a desktop computer, symbolizing the company's focus on technology and their expertise in providing technology-based solutions. Overall, the logo encapsulates the essence of RCT's mission to transform the African technology landscape through cutting-edge solutions and exceptional customer service.
